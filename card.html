Bengal Tiger

Bengal tiger is a subspecies of tiger, found accross Indian subcontinent. They are considered to be endangered species with population of around 2500.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris tigris
  • Average Weight: 250 kg
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Running Speed: 96 km/h
  • Habitat: Dense forests

Human that hunt Bengal tiger and habitat loss are the only threats to them. Protecting them means protecting forests, water resources, other animals, traditions. Governments are expected to own some in humanmade forests to protect their future.
